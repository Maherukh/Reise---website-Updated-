What's new in Reise?

Reise is more than just an App. How?

We at Reise went through a lot of applications which were related to travel. We say all apps are good but with some issues, so with a love of Travel we designed our own app meant to be not an app but a Companion. Yes, a virtual Companion which is for every traveler out there.

We can classify the following apps to be of two types
1.Travel Application
2.Travel Companion

1.Travel Application is something people use for direction or like a to-do list which has places marked on it to check in and out. Ex: Maps, Google Travels, Tripit etc..

2.Travel Companion is an app which gives a person which will show you around the place. Ex: Crouchsurfing, Meetup etc..

We made an app which behaves like a Companion but is actually an app

What we offer you..In a nutshell

A list of things/features you will get through this application

Discover the lesser known places

With our app you can discover places which are not very famous but interesting in their own sense, places which a guide or an app will not show as trending or mostly-visited. We have our own customized list of places which you can visit at your own pace and enjoy them just the way you like.

Travel at your own pace

Want to watch the beauty of a place for a little longer no problem, we allow our users to enjoy the beauty of travelling at their own pace, allowing them to enjoy every movement with-out any gimmick.

Explore the unseen

Checkout places which we researched for you and enjoy a good stay there, Every city has a place which is underrated and probably never as much visited as usually the others try our app to find the most underrated places.

Few other features you get..

A Planner

More like a schedule which adjusts to your inputs want to visit a place tomorrow we are there for you with our optimal and curated schedule.

Places near by

Find amazing places near you. We use Google's places API which allows us to find intresting places around you.

Find near by Hotels and restaurants

Hungry while travelling, we got it covered too just open up the app and enjoy all the amazing hotels and restaurants near you. Vegan we got it covered too with our vegan filter on restaurants.

Experience not a place but a city

Try every good thing about a city. Food, Buildings etc. and take a beautiful experience with you.
